Welcome to Wisdom Furnace

Image credit: Unsplash

Welcome to Wisdom Furnace

In addition to welcoming you to this page, I want to express my gratitude. Thank you for reading my words and being willing to listen to why I started this website.

I’m 27 years old this year, but I feel that my heart doesn’t seem as mature as a 27-year-old. The old saying goes, “At thirty, one should stand firm”, I still have three years to go, but I don’t perceive such ability.

With the passage of time and the increase of age, I’m not willing to extinguish my enthusiasm in daily idleness and repetition. I don’t want to be a person who is confused, doesn’t know where he came from, and even more doesn’t know where to go.

This website will act as a thumbtack📌, pinning down information, knowledge, and thoughts. It will constantly remind me to think about three questions: where we come from, what changes we are in now, and where we should go in the future.

Content Section Planning

This website will initially focus on several sections. I hope to combine what I’ve learned and consult with professionals to learn and share the following contents:

  1. Health-related knowledge, including common diseases and prevention, daily exercise injury protection, healthy diet and nutritional supplement suggestions, mental health maintenance, etc., to guide us to lay a solid foundation for a happy life;

  2. Some news, basic knowledge, and interesting cases in the field of economics and finance, guiding us to pursue economic independence and financial freedom;

  3. Some interesting cases about Chinese law, letting everyone know and understand the law, and become a person capable of protecting their own rights;

  4. Interesting tips for personal growth, life planning, time management, memory learning skills, etc.

  5. To be determined

The writing principles of these contents are: truth, objectivity, and beneficial

Expectations for This Website

I hope to keep it updated and let it become an important carrier to record the life trajectory and cognitive changes of me and my friends. I also hope this website can provide a place for my insightful friends who have many interesting stories to share but don’t have a platform, a place where they can chat, write, and share their opinions. Because I always believe, ideas become more interesting when they collide, and knowledge only generates value when shared and disseminated.

In the future, I hope to share our thoughts in more diverse forms.

Welcome👏🏻 Vision
Gilbert Yim
A Doctor in Shanghai,China.
